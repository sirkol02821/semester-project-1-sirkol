Join the hex-hunt!

There are thousands of hexagons hidden all over the museum! They can be found in and around the exhibits, on the floors, windows, ceilings, windows, in furniture, they are everywhere! Receive your own hexagon check-booklet at the entrance and see how many you are able to find during your next visit.

Until your next visit why not practice finding hexagons in your environment?
